As technology evolves, so do the threats that target digital systems. Cybersecurity has become one of the most critical areas in IT, focusing on protecting networks, data, and devices from malicious attacks.

Cyberattacks can cause severe financial and reputational damage to individuals and organizations. Common threats include malware, phishing, and ransomware. To combat these, cybersecurity experts use firewalls, encryption, two-factor authentication, and other defense mechanisms to secure information systems.

With the rise of remote work and cloud-based services, cybersecurity has become a shared responsibility. IT professionals must stay updated on new threats, while users must practice safe online habits to protect personal and organizational data.
