Cloud computing allows businesses and individuals to store and access data and programs over the internet instead of local computers. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost efficiency, making it one of the fastest-growing IT services today.

With cloud platforms like Amazon Web Services (AWS), Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure, organizations can easily scale their computing power and data storage needs. Employees can access files and collaborate in real time, no matter where they are located.

Cloud computing also enhances disaster recovery and data security. However, companies must ensure proper configurations and access controls to avoid data breaches and vulnerabilities.
